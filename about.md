### 视频录制参数

    参数                        值
    ---------------------------------------
    Frames Per Second           10
    Encode One the Fly          ✓
    Zero Compression            ✓
    Quick Subsampling           ✗
    Full shots at every frame   ✓

### 视频编码命令
